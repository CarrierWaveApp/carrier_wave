# POTA spot lookup

The Spots feature of Polo is awesome for Park2Park. I can just hit their spot from the side bar and it automatically changes the current logging frequency to theirs and marks as a P2P. Then when i hit enter it goes back to the freq I was previously on. Makes for a very seamless experience when I’m activating and just want to go get a P2P right quick. Would something like that be in the scope?

We need to create a view that's easily accessible from the logger that shows all POTA, SOTA, WWFF, and other spots. It should default to filtering on the logger's current mode if there's an active session, with a hint showing why it's filtering. Tapping on a spot should update the park, name, frequency, etc of the spot in the logger if there's an active session.
